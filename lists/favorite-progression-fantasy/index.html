Favorite Progression Fantasy series

2023-06-23

List of my favorite fantasy series that fall under the progression subgenre. The links lead to the series page on goodreads.

Completed series

5 stars aren't enough to gush about these series

  • Cradle by Will Wight
  • Mage Errant by John Bierce

5 stars

  • Super Powereds by Drew Hayes
  • Street Cultivation by Sarah Lin

4 stars

  • How to Defeat a Demon King in Ten Easy Steps by Andrew Rowe (standalone)
  • Traveler's Gate by Will Wight
  • Morcster Chef by Actus
  • New Game Minus by Sarah Lin
  • The Mage of Shimmer Mountain by Adam Sampson
  • The Rogue Dungeon by James A. Hunter
  • Threadbare by Andrew Seiple (only read first three books so far, which felt like a completed trilogy)
  • Euphoria Online by Phil Tucker
  • The Divine Dungeon by Dakota Krout (first three were great, last two were disappointing)

5 stars

  • Manifestation by Samuel Hinton
  • The Sharded Few by Alec Hutson
