Review

I read "Three Mages and a Margarita" (part of the connected series) last year. I had enjoyed that one too, but given my chaotic TBR, it took me until now to read this. "Warping Minds & Other Misdemeanors" can be read on its own, but it does spoil a few details. Visit the author's site for the recommended reading order.

Most of my impressions from Three Mages were applicable here too. A fast paced, fun and light hearted read that can be finished in less than a day. I enjoyed Kit's POV (even though I didn't get 95% of the film references) and the slow tease/reveal about his magical power was done well. I liked Lienna's character too, would've liked better if some of the scenes were from her POV.

I suppose romance and focus on physical attractions are common tropes in urban fantasy. I don't mind romantic banter, but I wanted to roll my eyes like Lienna at the physical descriptions and teasing present in almost every chapter. Probably one of the reasons why I'm not binge reading the gazillion books in the "The Guild Codex" series. However, the next time I desperately need a fast paced read, I hope my unmanageable TBR will surface this series among the top choices.
